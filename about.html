Smart Home Lighting System

About Our Smart Home Lighting System

The Smart Home Lighting System provides seamless control of your home lights through a simple web interface. Whether you're on your phone or laptop, you can turn your lights on or off with a single click.

The system uses ESP32 microcontroller technology, which connects to your Wi-Fi network and controls the lights. The system's status is synced with the Firebase Realtime Database, which allows for instant updates whenever you interact with the website.

Technology Behind the System

ESP32 Microcontroller

The ESP32 is a powerful Wi-Fi and Bluetooth microcontroller that connects your home lighting to the internet, enabling remote control through a web-based interface.

Firebase Realtime Database

Firebase acts as the backbone of our system. It provides a cloud-based, real-time database that stores the status of your lights, allowing instant updates when you press the On/Off buttons from the website.
